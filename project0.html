Alai Devanagari
TYPEFACE

Alai is a rounded san-serif that aims to combine the gently curved elements of a Devanagari typeface with the more modern and unique features of a Latin typeface. This typeface was created with its Devanagari counterpart in mind, which is currently being worked on.
