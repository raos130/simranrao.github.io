DIRECTOR'S EDITION

A series of publications highlighting some of my favorite directors and trying to encapsulate their style and common themes that recur in their films in the design of each publication.
Created with Indesign

ALFRED HITCHCOCK

This publication was inspired by the posters of some of Hitchcock's most iconic films, Vertigo and Just Dial M for Murder, as well as his themes of danger and suspense, which I represented with bold colors and text. The narrow columns of text are reminescent of his simple yet dynamic style of story-telling, making something interesting from very few elements, as seen in Rear Window.

DENIS VILLENEUVE

Villeneuve is known for his dark, psychological thrillers complete with unexpected plot twist. To encapsulate the tone, I used a dark color palette with a white to create a vivid contrast. The flipped text plays with his themes of chaos and twist endings, as seen in Enemy and Prisoners as well as the introspective nature of the film. His films are known for their strong visual aesthetics, and I tried to create my own stark color palette for this publication.

CHRISTOPHER NOLAN

This publication was inspired by Nolan's signature style of film-making. To represent his themes of identity and twins, as seen in Inception and The Prestige, I used multiple fonts and contrasted each alternate page to create a dynamic sense of reptition. Another staple element in his films, is his non-linear way of story-telling. The offset emphasised text is a break from this publications set guide of narrating content.
