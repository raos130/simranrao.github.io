Windspeed
HTML/CSS/JS

This webpage was inspired by the idea of visually displaying what temperature looks like abstractly and linearly, based on what it makes an individual feel. The site is linked to a weather API that shows the changes in windspeeds around the world. The faster the speed of wind in real-time, the quicker the lines move, and the more saturated the colors get.
