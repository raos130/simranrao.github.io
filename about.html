ABOUT ME

Simran Rao is a designer from India, based in New York City. She is presently studying Communication Design at Parsons School of Design. Her lie in Branding, Motion Graphics, Typeface Design and Editorial Design.
Apart from design, she has a huge passion for films. Growing up in Mumbai, she has been influenced by films from the Eastern as well as Western culture. Currently, she is working on the Devanagari counterpart to my Latin typeface, Alai.
