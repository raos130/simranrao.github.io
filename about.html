ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Simran Rao and I'm a designer from India, based in New York City. I'm presently studying Communication Design at Parsons School of Design. My interests lie in UI/UX, Branding, Motion Graphics, and Editorial Design.
Apart from Design, I have a huge passion for films. Growing up in Mumbai, I've been influenced by films from the Eastern as well as Western culture. Currently, I'm working on a three-part editorial publication that critiques misogyny in Bollywood.
